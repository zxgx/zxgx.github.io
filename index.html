I am a PhD student at Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo. I received Master and Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Wuhan University in 2022 and Hubei University in 2019, respectively.

My current interests lie in designing security systems and improving software quality with the help of program analysis, machine learning and deep learning techniques. Currently, I mainly focus on program analysis and blockchain security.

Research Interests

Blockchain; Smart Contract; Software Engineering; Natural Language Processing
