I am a master's student at School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China. I received Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Wuhan University in 2020.

My current research interest is in developing scalable and performant algorithms and systems for machine learning. Previously, I also did research in knowledge graph question answering (KGQA) with natural language processing techniques.

From May to Aug, 2022, I worked as an intern at HPC-AI Technology Inc., and contributed to the large-scale model training system, Colossal-AI.
